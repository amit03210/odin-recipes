Dahi Puri

dahi puri recipe | how to make dahi batata puri recipe with detailed photo and video recipe. a popular indian street food chaat recipe which is prepared very similar to sev puri, but filled with sweet yogurt and other chaat chutneys.

it is typically consumed as evening snack and perhaps as a dessert after other chaat recipes.

Ingredients

  • 6 puris / papdis
  • ½ potato, boiled & peeled
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Black mustard seeds ½ onion, finely chopped
  • ½ to mato, finely chopped
  • 5 tsp tamarind chutney
  • 3 tsp green chutney
  • pinch of kashmiri chili powder
  • pinch of chaat masala
  • salt to taste
  • 3 tsp coriander leaves, finely chopped

Instructions

  1. firstly, make a hole at the centre of puri with your thumb.
  2. further, stuff half tsp of boiled potatoes into each puris.
  3. furthermore, take a cup of curd and mix sugar in it.
  4. also add generous amount of sweet curd into each puris.
  5. sprinkle generous amount of chopped onions.
  6. also sprinkle and spread chopped tomatoes.
  7. furthermore, top with generous amount of thin sev.
  8. then pour and spread tamarind chutney.
  9. further add green chutney onto each puri.
  10. then also top with a tsp of curd.
  11. sprinkle chili powder, chaat masala and black salt or cooking salt.
  12. finally, garnish with coriander leaves and serve sev puri immediately.
